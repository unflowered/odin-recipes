This is how you make homemade pasta!

Home

Here the the ingredients you need.

  • two cups of all purpose flour
  • three eggs
  • two tablespoons of olive oil
  • a teaspoon of salt
  • water

    • Here are the steps to make the pasta

    1. Mix flour, eggs, olive oil, and salt in a bowl until combined. Add one tb of water at a time until smooth and thick.
    2. Throw dough onto a lightly floured work surface and knead for 10 minutes. Let dough rest for 5 to 10 minutes
    3. Divide dough into 8 balls; use a pasta machine to roll and cut dough into desired shape

    Now you get to enjoy your homemade pasta and use it in any recipe you desire!
