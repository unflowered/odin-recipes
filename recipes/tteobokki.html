This is how you make tteobokki (Korean Rice Cakes)!

Home

Here the the ingredients you need.

  • three cups water
  • two dried anchovies to taste
  • three tablespoons chile paste
  • two tablespoons white sugar
  • one tablespoon soy sauce
  • two korean fish cakes, sliced
  • half an onion, thickly sliced
  • one spring onion, thickly sliced

    • Here are the steps to make the pasta

    1. Combine water and achovies in a saucepan and bring a boil. Cook for 10 minutes. Remove and discard anchovies. Set anchovy water aside.
    2. Combine chile paste, sugar, soy sauce, and corn syrup in a bowl. Set chile paste mixture aside.
    3. Add rice rackes, onion, and chile paste mixture to anchovy water. Bring to a boil for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add spring onion and boil 3 more minutes.

    Now go enjoy your spicy rice cakes!
